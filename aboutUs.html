About Us

Seventy years bringing floral arrangements

To the city of Limerick


Mill flowers have been in exixtence since the 1950's. Three generations of our family have worked in the family business, artistically hand- arranging flowers for every ocassions. We provide services from everyday bouquets, birthday and holiday gift baskets, funeral baskets, special ocassion arrangements and more! No matter what the ocassion, Mill flowers will put every ounce of love, dedication and personalization to every piece that is created by us.
